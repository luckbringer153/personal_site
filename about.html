Hello!

I received my Bachelor of Science from Oregon State University with a major in industrial engineering and a minor in food technology. My original goal was to pursue a plant manager role at any large-size food production company. In service to this, I completed two engineering internships with Glanbia Nutritionals and one with E. & J. Gallo Winery. I learned that I'm very good at adapting to new development processes quickly when a better way of doing things presents itself. I strive to prioritize the customer (internal or external) in any of my projects, especially in the areas of communication, usability, and longevity. Lastly, I learned that I love diving deep into the troubleshooting stages of a project - it's like a big puzzle! Fortunately I had the opportunity to create multiple macro-enabled Excel workbooks with Visual Basic for Applications (VBA), and it was there I discovered that my true passion lied in coding.

In pursuit of a technical role, I transitioned to my current position as a Business Analyst II with Insight Global. Working remotely, I've honed my communication and project organization skills through projects with Pacific Gas and Electric (PG&E) and Chevron. I've audited over 60,000 wildfire ignition reports, prototyped improved processes for incoming data processing, and balanced throughness with efficiency for our high-priority projects. In the past year, I've grown my coding "toolbelt" by learning and practicing new coding languages, the first of these being Transcript-SQL via a two-month SQL certificate course offered by the Learning Resources Network (LERN). The second was the completion of a web development certificate offered by Fullstack Academy through San Jose State University. This 12-week course taught me full-stack development fundamentals, including front-end (HTML, CSS) and back-end (JS, React, NodeJS, PostgreSQL) development. This course culminated in a capstone project wherein me and two students developed a fully-functional e-commerce website in less than two weeks.

Currently, I am a full-stack engineer seeking an entry-level role in web or database design. My background may be "unconventional" in software engineering, but it has given me accelerated exposure to customer satisfaction, the design-to-prototype-to-deployment life cycle, and to engaging the strengths of the whole team. I thrive in the development process!

Outside of work, I love to cook, bake, and grow hot peppers. Most evenings you'll find me line dancing or tinkering with personal projects. I primarly code in Visual Studio Code, using the Ubuntu terminal to connect with GitHub repositories. View my GitHub or the "My Portfolio" section for information about my previous and current projects.

I look forward to meeting with you! My contact information can be found on my resume (attached below) or through my LinkedIn.
